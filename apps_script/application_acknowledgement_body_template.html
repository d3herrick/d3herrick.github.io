Thank you for submitting an application to the Newton Tree Conservancy's Community Tree Planting program. If your application requested inclusion in an established neighborhood planting group, the director from the Newton Tree Conservancy who is assigned to that planting group will be in touch with you as we approach the next planting day.

The Newton Tree Conservancy plants in neighborhood groups that include 8 or more trees to be planted in the empty spots of 5 or more homeowners who live within a few blocks of each other. If by the application deadline for the next planting date, the planting group you specified in your application does not include at least 8 trees and 5 homeowners, the Newton Tree Conservancy may retain your application to include it in a future planting when a planting group forms in close proximity to your address.

Note: The Newton Tree Conservancy will install a watering bag on each tree you receive. You must agree to water those trees by filling their watering bags from mid-April to mid-November for two years. If you, or a family member, friend or neighbor, are unable to fulfill this requirement, please reply to this email to let the Newton Tree Conservancy know that it should remove your application from consideration. For additional information, see Requirements.
